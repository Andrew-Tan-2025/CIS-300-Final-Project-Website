What is SuperGiant Games?

Founded in 2009, SuperGiant Games is an indie game studio founded by Amir Rao & Gavin Simon, based in San Jose, CA. Their games are defined by:

  • Narrative-based gameplay, with...
  • Innovative Gameplay mechanics, and an...
  • Emphasis on Story & Character writing

While definitely not the biggest fan, I would say that I enjoy the games this studio has made. From the unique mechanics to the charming dialogue & well-written cast of colorful characters, all the games that SuperGiant has created occupy a special place in my heart.

This website is not only for me to talk about why I enjoy these games, but also to hopefully convince anyone who sees this to give their games a try!
